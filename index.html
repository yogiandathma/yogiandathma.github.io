About the couple...

Athma

Athma was born and raised in Stamford, CT, perfecting her speed reading skills. After hopping around between schools named after states and cities, she has lived in Cambridge, MA for 4 years. She likes to navigate maps in search of ice cream, hide tiny origami, and make perfect snow angels. When Athma first met Yogi, she was impressed at how many hobbies he had.

Yogi

Yogi was born in Bangalore, India and grew up in Kent, OH searching for the ideal Kent mango. After hopping around between schools named after states and cities, he has been back in Cambridge, MA for 3 years. He likes to climb trees, blog about Indian food, and pretend to be an 80s rock star. When Yogi first met Athma, he was impressed by her ability to pick perfect kumquats.
