Indian Chai Tea - Black or Red

1) Steep entire bottle's contents in 5 cups of simmering water or milk for ~15 minutes.

2) Sweeten with sugar if desired.

3) Drink as concentrate for strong tea or dilute 1:2 with water for milder tea. Keep refrigerated for up to one week.


To make individual cups, mix entire bottle's contents and use two teaspoons of tea per cup.


Ingredients: Assam Black Tea or Rooibus Red Tea, Sugar, Cardomom Pods, Crystallized Ginger, Cloves, Fennel, Cordiander, Black Pepper, Cinnamon, Star Anise



Mango Black Tea

1) Steep entire bottle's contents in 5 cups of simmering water for ~15 minutes.

2) Sweeten with sugar or honey if desired.

3) Drink as concentrate for strong tea or dilute 1:2 with water for milder tea. Keep refrigerated for up to one week.


To make individual cups, mix entire bottle's contents and use two teaspoons of tea per cup.


Ingredients: Assam Black Tea, Sugar, Crystallized Ginger, Dried Mango, Hibiscus Leaves, Gogi Berries
