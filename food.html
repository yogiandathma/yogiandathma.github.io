For your dining pleasure...

Some of our favorite places to eat, drink, and treat ourselves are listed below. If you want more recommendations, please e-mail us!

To Eat...


Dosa-n-Curry Glorious South Indian food, all vegetarian and temple style. Under-appreciated at a 4 samosa rating.

Arlen & Harlow The golden child of Cambridge small plates, accessibly hip dining. Good atmosphere and drinks, lots to share!

Helmand Traditional Afghan cuisine with a hidden gem quality.

Aceituna Made-to-order Mediterrean/Lebanese platters and sandwiches, long lunch lines, delicious flies, and superbly garlicky

Clover Multiple locations throughout Cambridge serving up quick vegetarian plates/pitas. Local, fresh, fair produce abounds.


To Drink...


Cafe Art Science Molecular drink-o-nomy at its finest. Sit at the bar and ask about their methods, machinery, and reagents!

Lamplighter New-kid-in-town local yeast brewery. Hipster Cambridge at its best, with fine funky beers.


To Indulge...


Toscanini's Perfect combination of gritty yet creamy ice cream. Inventive twists on classic ingredients, bold flavors, always a line out the door (even in winter!).

Mike's Pastry Italian pastries come to Cambridge - their cannolis are sought after throughout the country (or so say the crowds who never seem to reduce)

L.A. Burdick Cambridge instituion for all things fine chocolate.
